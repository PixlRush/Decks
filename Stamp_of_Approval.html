Deck Goals

  • Sustain powerful friends through means of placing cards on top of your deck and drawing cards
  • Control and Remove big threats to the deck
  • Go through your deck as fast as possible to find the combo peices.

Comboes

  • Mayor Mare or Moondancer with Meticulous X or Starswirl Research
  • Babs Seed with any form of draw, mainly Let's Get This Party Started
  • Scope Things Out is a great enabler for Babs, Moodnancer, and Mayor Mare.

Entry

  • Babs Seed takes care of Pink entry for me. She's under-curve the turn she enters play (2 power for 3 AT), but she more than makes up for it later on. Pink being a splash for some tech cards later on needs no more entry than the 3 babs provides.
  • Mayor Mare and Owlowliscious takes care of my Purple entry seeing as purple is the main colour of the deck and the colour on which this strategy revolves around. Mayor Mare is there as a turn 1 play, and a way to start me on my way to my combo. Owlowliscious is useful in that he's a 2-drop for no requirements that will also pump up your Mayor Mares and Moondancers.

Lines of Play

Here are some good lines of play to use in game. They generally function to serve the combo and make use of the combo.

  • Opener

    1. Play Mayor Mare to the lowest requirement on a problem. Leave 1 AT for next turn.
    2. Play Moondancer to the other more hefty requirement on a problem. Then at the end of your opponent's turn play Starswirl Research, Twilight flips.
    3. Use Twilight's Meticulous 1 and put the card (Starswirl Research) on top of your deck Mayor Mare and Moondancer get +1 and +2 respectively. Play a second Mayor Mare to the same problem as the first, if you have none in hand play a 2-drop (preferably Owlowliscious). You can then score 1 point, or 2 and force a DFO. Using Starswirl is optional here.

  • Upkeep Boost (Needs: Starswirl Research in hand or on top of your deck, Spell Showdown as your active problem)

    1. If Starswirl Research is in your hand, put it on top of your deck before the beginning of turn triggers fire.
    2. Trigger Spell Showdown and reveal Starswirl Research giving them no information. Put it into your hand.
    3. Resolve your Meticulous triggers to buff, dig for an answer, or prepare for your next turn.
    4. Optional: Replay Starswirl Research before you draw your card for an extra buff to your purple combo peices.
    5. You now have a big set of buffs all for 0 AT.

  • Fully Stacked (Needs: 1 AT, Scope Things Out. Benefits from Spell Showdown, Starswirl Research)

    1. If Starswirl Research is in your hand, put it on top of your deck before the beginning of turn triggers fire.
    2. Pay 1 AT to play Scope Things Out
      • If you have dead cards in hand you want to cycle away and have enough Meticulous to cycle them to the bottom, place those cards further up on the deck.
      • If you have Spell Showdown put an event on the top of your deck, or the first card under the dead cards being cycled away.
      • Then stack your deck for maybe desirable Chaos effects (at least 2 cards down) and leave a card you want to draw on top.
    3. After Scope Things Out resolves your mane will have been flipped. You resolve your Meticulous in accordance with the previous list.
